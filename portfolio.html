2.) "Lister"

Developed by myself and my Codercamps classmates Joe Waynick and Suzanne Federico. Using the "Agile Methodology", we worked as a small Dev Team and created this very powerful business tool! Lister is a web app that can be used by any online entrepreneur who wishes to leverage the power of Amazon to sell products. Lister interfces with Amazon's complex API and makes it much faster and easier to upload and list your products for sale. This app will save you many hours weekly, and free up your time to do all of the other important business processes you might have on your agenda. Technologies used: MS Visual Studio, HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript (Angular library), Typescript, and C#.
